Simple

The future simple, also known as the simple future tense, is a verb tense used to express actions or events that will happen in the future.

  • I will go to the party tomorrow.
  • She will start her new job next week.
Continious

The future continuous, also known as the future progressive, is a verb tense used to express actions or events that will be happening at a specific point in the future.

  • I will be studying for my exams tomorrow evening.
  • She will be working on a project all day.
Perfect

The future perfect is a verb tense used to express actions or states that will be completed before a specific point in the future.

  • By the time you arrive, I will have finished my work.
  • She will have graduated from university by next year.
